# Nana's Guacamole

**Ingredients:**
- 2 avocados
- 1 lime
- 2 tsp salt
- jalapeno to taste
- 1/2 onion
- 5 sprigs cilantro

Instructions:
- cut and mash avocados
- chop onion
- chop jalapeno
- squeeze lime
- add salt
- and mix well
